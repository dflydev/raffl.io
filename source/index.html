raffl.io

Easy to Manage Raffling

Do you do giveaways at conferences or tradeshows? Do you ever multiple prizes that people are signing up for individually? Having a hard time keeping track of who signed up for what?

Raffl.io is a simple hosted service that manages raffled items and tracks which of recipients are interested in each item.

We're currently under development. Want early access? Sign up for the raffl.io mailing list to find out how!
